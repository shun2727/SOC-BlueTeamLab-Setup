Incident Summary

Multiple failed SSH authentication attempts were detected against a Linux server. The volume and frequency of login failures indicate a potential brute-force attack targeting user credentials.

The purpose of this investigation is to:

Identify the source IP address(es).
Determine the targeted user account(s).
Verify whether any authentication attempts were successful.
Assess whether the attacker established persistence or performed additional malicious activities after login.
Recommend appropriate containment and remediation actions.

Affected Service

SSH (Port 22)

Primary Log Sources

/var/log/auth.log
Wazuh alerts
Firewall logs (if available)

MITRE ATT&CK

T1110 – Brute Force
